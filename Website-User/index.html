About Us

Vaibhav Budhwant and Associates

Your Trusted Partner for Construction Approvals & Land Documentation

Vaibhav Budhwant and Associates is a reliable and experienced consultancy firm offering complete solutions for construction permissions, architectural planning, land documentation, and government approvals. With a commitment to professionalism, accuracy, and timely execution, we help homeowners, builders, and landowners complete their legal and technical processes smoothly and without complications.

From obtaining construction permissions to preparing detailed building plans, handling Gunthewari regularization, TDR services, NA 44 layouts, and various government NOCs, our expert team provides end-to-end guidance at every step.

Our goal is to simplify complex procedures and provide transparent, efficient, and trustworthy services to our clients.
